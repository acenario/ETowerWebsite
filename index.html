Our History

        E-Tower was founded in 2001 by Andrew Foley (Co-founder of IdeaPaint). His mission was to bring together the best student entrepreneurs into one living community at Babson College. Babson is renowned as the #1 undergraduate school for entrepreneurship for over 15 years.
       Through its development ETower became a highly valued space on campus. Special guests and unique events would attract students from all over to ETower's lounge. Many residents have started companies that have raised millions in sales and have been featured in Bloomberg Businessweek, TechCrunch, and other notable news sources.
